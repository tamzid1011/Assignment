Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Creating new ideas and using technology to grow. It helps solve problems and improve lives.

Sustainability

Protecting the environment and using resources wisely. It ensures a better future for the next generations.

Infrastructure

Building strong and modern systems for development. It supports businesses and improves daily life.

Empowerment

Giving everyone the chance to succeed and be strong. It promotes equality and boosts community strength.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 is about building a better future with technology, clean energy, and equal opportunities for everyone. The goal is to make the country stronger and more successful, creating a brighter future for all its people.

Digital Transformation

"Bangladesh 2.0" refers to the country's digital transformation, focusing on e-government, fintech, e-commerce, education, healthcare, and infrastructure. With initiatives like Digital Bangladesh Vision 2021, the goal is to foster economic growth, innovation, and inclusivity through advanced technology adoption.

Green Energy

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to embrace green energy through renewable sources like solar, wind, and hydro power, reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainable development for a cleaner, more energy-efficient future.reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainable development for a cleaner, more.

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on modern infrastructure development, including smart cities, improved transportation, digital connectivity, and sustainable buildings, aiming to enhance urban living, boost economic growth, and ensure efficient public services.boost economic growth, and ensure efficient public services.

Bangladesh 2.0 is a vision for the future, where the country can lead the world with innovation, creativity, and social business. I believe that if we focus on social business, we can eliminate poverty and bring about change in society. This is not just a model for Bangladesh, but for the entire world. By giving people the opportunity to solve their own problems, we can build a society where everyone has a chance to succeed, no one is left behind, and a just and strong nation is created.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
